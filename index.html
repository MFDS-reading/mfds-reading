Mathematical Foundations of Data Science Reading Group

We are a group of graduate students from Departments of Statistics, Computer Sciences, Electrical and Computer Engineering at UW-Madison, advised by professors Po-Ling Loh and Varun Jog.

This reading group is an informal weekly meeting to discuss advanced mathematical tools, which are widely applied in data science.

Schedule

For the Spring 2018 term, we meet on Mondays, 4pm–5:30pm in WID 1153. The topic of this semester is Random Matrix Theory, we'll discuss Terence Tao's notes.
