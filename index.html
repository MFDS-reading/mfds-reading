Mathematical Foundations of Data Science Reading Group

We are a group of graduate students from Departments of Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Sciences, Electrical and Computer Engineering at UW-Madison, advised by professors Po-Ling Loh and Varun Jog.

This reading group is an informal weekly meeting to discuss advanced mathematical tools, which are widely applied in data science.
